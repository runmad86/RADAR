# TeamFormSummary

## Rolle
Afledt opsummering af et holds nyere adfaerd.

## Felter
- team_id
- sample_size
- goals_scored_rate
- goals_conceded_rate
- btts_rate
- over25_rate
- yellow_card_rate
- red_card_rate
- updated_at

## Formaal
TeamFormSummary goer signalmotoren hurtigere og renere ved at samle nyere holdform i et lille lag oven paa fakta-tabellerne.

## Regel
TeamFormSummary er afledt lag. Ikke dybeste sandhed.
