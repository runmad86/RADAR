# Match

## Rolle
Kerneobjektet for en kamp.

## Felter
- id
- external_match_id
- competition_id
- competition_name
- season_label
- match_datetime
- home_team_id
- away_team_id
- home_team_name
- away_team_name
- home_score_ft
- away_score_ft
- home_score_ht
- away_score_ht
- status
- last_updated_at

## Formaal
Match er kampens identitet og faelles reference for resten af RADAR.

## Regel
Match er fakta. Ikke fortolkning.
